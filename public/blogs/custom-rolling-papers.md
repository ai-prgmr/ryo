Production runs with inline GSM monitoring (±5% tolerance), batch quality control, and Certificate of Analysis issued for the shipment.

Full export documentation in-house: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin (with FTA preference where eligible), COA, compliance declarations. FOB shipment from Indian port.

## Common Questions About Custom Rolling Papers

### What is the minimum order quantity for custom rolling papers?
RYO Papers' MOQ is **50,000 booklets per SKU** for fully customized production — significantly lower than most international rolling paper manufacturers, who typically require 100,000–250,000 booklet MOQs. For multi-SKU launches, MOQ applies per SKU.

### How long does custom rolling paper production take?
**40–60 days** order-to-FOB at our port. This includes sample approval (7–14 days), artwork finalization (5–10 days), production run (12–18 days), QC + packaging (5–7 days), and export documentation (3–5 days). Sea freight transit adds 18–35 days depending on destination.

### Can I customize the watermark to my brand logo?
Yes. Custom watermarks are physically embedded into the paper during sheet formation using a **dandy roll** with your custom design. The watermark is part of the fiber structure — visible when held to light. A one-time tooling fee applies (typically $300–$800 depending on design complexity).

### What custom GSM values are available?
Any GSM between **10 and 35**. The most-ordered custom GSM specs are 14, 16, 18, and 22 GSM. We maintain ±5% GSM tolerance per batch as standard, with ±3% available on request for export-grade orders.

### Can I order custom hemp + rice blends?
Yes. Custom blend ratios are available — 70:30, 60:40, 50:50, or any custom ratio. Hemp + rice blends are the **most-requested custom specification**, combining hemp's sustainability story with rice's smoothness. [Read the hemp vs rice comparison](/blogs/hemp-vs-rice-rolling-papers).

### What's the price per booklet for custom rolling papers?
FOB India pricing depends on fiber tier, GSM, packaging, and order volume:
- **Wood pulp economy:** $0.08–$0.15 per booklet
- **Hemp premium:** $0.15–$0.30 per booklet
- **Rice luxury:** $0.20–$0.40 per booklet
- **Premium custom blends with full custom packaging:** $0.25–$0.50 per booklet

[Request your exact quotation →](/contact)

### Do you handle custom packaging too?
Yes. Custom packaging covers all three layers: **booklet cover** (200-300 GSM stock, full-color print, foil/embossing), **display box** (24 or 50 booklets, fully printed), and **master carton** (custom shipping marks). Eco-packaging options available.

### Can I order custom rolling papers if I'm new to the industry?
Yes — RYO Papers regularly works with first-time founders. We provide guidance on spec selection, packaging design, compliance requirements, and launch logistics. [Read our complete guide to launching a rolling paper brand](/blogs/how-to-start-rolling-paper-brand).

### Do you ship custom rolling papers internationally?
Yes. Regular exports to North America, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, ASEAN, ANZ, and Latin America. Full export documentation included. [Contact us](/contact) to confirm logistics for your destination.

### Can I get samples before committing to a custom order?
Yes. We provide **pre-production samples** in your target fiber/GSM range so you can verify burn, feel, color, and quality before placing a production order. Sample fees apply (typically refundable against your first production order).
