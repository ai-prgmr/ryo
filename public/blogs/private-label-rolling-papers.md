Premium hemp fiber from sustainable industrial hemp. Slow burn, neutral taste, eco-credentials buyers recognize. Available 14–25 GSM, bleached or unbleached.

### 🌾 Rice Rolling Papers
Ultra-thin rice fiber for connoisseur and luxury brands. The cleanest burn, lowest ash, most refined experience. Available 10–14 GSM.

### 🌱 Flax (Linen) Rolling Papers
The traditional French paper fiber — heritage positioning, exceptional tensile strength. Available 13–18 GSM.

### 📜 Wood Pulp Rolling Papers
Cost-effective mass-market production. TCF or ECF chlorine-free options available. Reliable supply at competitive pricing. 18–28 GSM.

### 🔀 Custom Fiber Blends
The most-requested specification — **hemp + rice blends** combining sustainability storytelling with ultra-thin smoothness. Any ratio: 70:30, 60:40, 50:50, custom.

## How The Private Label Process Works

A clear, predictable 6-stage process from first inquiry to your warehouse:

### Stage 1 — Discovery Call (Day 1–3)
You share your brand vision, target market, and rough specs. We share pricing ranges, MOQ, lead times, and recommendations.

### Stage 2 — Sampling (Day 5–18)
We send physical samples in your target fiber/GSM range so you can verify burn, feel, color, and quality before committing.

### Stage 3 — Spec Lock + Quotation (Day 18–25)
You finalize fiber, GSM, size, watermark, gum, packaging. We issue a formal FOB quotation with exact pricing.

Production runs with inline GSM monitoring, batch QC, and a Certificate of Analysis issued for every shipment.

### Stage 6 — Export + Dispatch (Day 55–60)
Full export documentation handled in-house: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin (with FTA preference where available), COA, compliance declarations. FOB shipment from Indian port.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the minimum order quantity for private label rolling papers?
RYO Papers' MOQ is **50,000 booklets per SKU** for fully customized private-label production — significantly lower than most international rolling paper manufacturers, who typically require 100,000–250,000 booklet MOQs. For multi-SKU launches, MOQ applies per SKU.

### What's the price per booklet?
FOB pricing depends on fiber tier, GSM, size, packaging, and order volume. Approximate ranges in 2026:
- **Wood pulp economy:** $0.08–$0.15 per booklet
- **Hemp premium:** $0.15–$0.30 per booklet
- **Rice luxury:** $0.20–$0.40 per booklet
- **Premium custom blends with full custom packaging:** $0.25–$0.50 per booklet

[Request your exact quotation →](/contact)

### How long does production take?
**40–60 days** order-to-FOB at our port. Sea freight transit adds 18–35 days depending on destination. Total order-to-warehouse is realistically **60–95 days**. Air freight available at 3–7 days globally for urgent orders.

### Do you handle export documentation?
Yes — fully in-house. Every shipment ships with: Commercial Invoice, Packing List, Bill of Lading, Certificate of Origin (with FTA preference where applicable), Certificate of Analysis (COA), Material Safety declarations, and compliance certificates for the destination market.

### What payment terms do you accept?
Standard terms for new clients: **30% advance, 70% before shipment.** We also accept Letters of Credit (LC) at sight, and bank wire transfer (TT). Repeat clients can negotiate to 50/50 or 30/30/40 terms based on order history.

### Can I visit the factory?
Yes — factory visits are welcomed. RYO Papers is located in Indore, Madhya Pradesh, India, well-connected via Indore Airport (IDR) with direct flights from Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore. Most international clients manage projects fully remotely, but on-site visits are encouraged for relationship-building.

### Do you ship to my country?
RYO Papers regularly exports to North America (USA, Canada), Europe (UK, Germany, France, Spain, Netherlands, Italy, etc.), the Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Kuwait, Oman), Africa (Nigeria, Kenya, South Africa, Morocco, Egypt), Asia-Pacific (Australia, New Zealand, Japan, Singapore), and Latin America. [Contact us](/contact) to confirm shipping logistics for your specific destination.

### Can I order samples before committing?
Yes. We provide pre-production samples in your target fiber/GSM range so you can verify the product before placing a production order. Sample shipping fees apply (typically refundable against your first production order).

### Do you offer design support?
We provide **dieline templates and technical specifications** for your design team. Full graphic design services are available on request through our partner network — but most clients work with their own designers.

### Are rolling papers regulated as tobacco or cannabis products?
No. Rolling papers are classified as **paper products** under international export law — not tobacco, not cannabis. This means standard import documentation is sufficient in most markets, with no tobacco/cannabis licensing requirements.
